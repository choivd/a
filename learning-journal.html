About This Site

Why I'm doing this

The goal

I'm a designer building deep expertise in how AI actually works — not just using it. Context engineering, system architecture, evaluation, knowledge design. The goal is to eventually design systems that include humans, AI, workflows, memory, and organizations together.

Why in public

Learning in public creates useful pressure. It forces clarity — you can't document something you don't actually understand. And it builds a visible trail of genuine thinking over time. This site is accountability infrastructure, not a portfolio.

Learning in Public Built to document, not to impress.
